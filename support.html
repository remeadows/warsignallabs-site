Frequently asked

Common questions.

The app crashes on launch or freezes during gameplay.

Force-close the app, restart your device, and relaunch. If the issue persists, ensure your iOS version is up to date and that you have at least 200 MB of free storage. If the problem continues after a reinstall, email us with your device model and iOS version.

I lost my progress after reinstalling the app.

Progress is stored on-device in the current version. Uninstalling the app deletes local save data. We recommend playing through iCloud-linked accounts when available. A cloud-save feature is planned for a future update.

How do I report a bug or submit feedback?

Email remeadows@warsignallabs.net with a description of the issue, your device model, your iOS version, and any steps to reproduce the problem. Screenshots or screen recordings are always helpful.

I was charged for an in-app purchase but didn’t receive it.

All purchases are processed by Apple. For billing issues, refund requests, or missing purchases, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. Apple handles all transaction disputes directly.
