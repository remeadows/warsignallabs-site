How we work

Observe. Analyze. Respond.

Every engagement runs the same loop. The pace differs; the discipline does not.

01 · Observe

Understand the system before changing it.

Inventory what exists. Map the data flows. Identify what is trusted, what is exposed, and what nobody quite remembers anymore.

02 · Analyze

Find the gaps that matter.

Threat-model against the real adversary. Prioritize by impact, not by how loud the alert is. Document assumptions so decisions can be challenged.

03 · Respond

Ship reversible changes with runbooks.

Improvements are made in steps that can be rolled back. Every change leaves the team with documentation and the ability to operate without us.
