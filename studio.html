FIELD NOTES

Why Studio

Network defense and music share more than aesthetic. Both are systems built from layered signals, mixed in real time, and judged on whether the listener — or the operator — can tell when something is off. Working in both keeps the same instinct sharp.

Studio output funds nothing critical and breaks no production. That's exactly why it's where the fastest experimentation happens — multi-agent orchestration, prompt iteration, mastering chains, distribution loops. The lessons travel.
